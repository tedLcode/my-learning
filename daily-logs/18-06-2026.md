- Topic: Architectural Decision-Making in Analytics Projects

- Topic: Snowflake as a Data Model for Analytics
  - What I understood: Choosing Snowflake (as a modeling pattern, not just a platform) means organizing data into normalized dimension tables connected to a central fact — this keeps the schema clean and queryable without needing an additional transformation layer on top
  - What I understood: Pairing Snowflake modeling with Python visualization keeps the stack lean — query the data in Snowflake, render the insights in Python, without introducing another tool or framework in between
